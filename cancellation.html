Cancellation and Refund Policy

At Seeru, we recognize that travel plans may change, and we are committed to providing our valued customers with flexible cancellation and refund options. Please take a moment to review our cancellation and refund policy outlined below.

Cancellation Fees

Cancellation fees may be applicable based on the policies of the airline, hotel, or other travel service provider. These fees are typically detailed during the booking process and can vary depending on factors such as fare type, booking class, and specific terms established by the provider.

Refund Eligibility

Eligibility for a refund is contingent upon the terms and conditions set forth by the respective travel service provider (e.g., airline, hotel, car rental agency). It's important to note that certain fares, particularly non-refundable ones, may not qualify for a refund.

Refund Process

To initiate a refund, kindly reach out to our Customer Happiness Team or use our live chat service. Our dedicated team will guide you through the process and assist in liaising with the relevant travel service provider.

Processing Time

Refund processing times vary and are determined by the policies of the specific travel service provider. While we make every effort to expedite the process, we ask for your understanding and patience, allowing a reasonable timeframe for the refund to be processed.

Flight Changes and Modifications

Some airlines may offer the option to make changes to your booking. Please be aware that additional fees and fare differences may apply. For assistance with modifications, kindly contact our customer support team.

Special Promotions and Offers

Special promotions or discounted fares may have unique cancellation and refund policies. These policies will be clearly communicated at the time of booking, ensuring transparency and clarity for our customers.

At Seeru, we are committed to providing excellent customer service, and we appreciate your understanding and cooperation with our cancellation and refund procedures. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please don't hesitate to reach out to our Customer Happiness Team at help@seeru.com. Safe travels!
